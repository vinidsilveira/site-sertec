# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Transport-refrigeration operators and fleet managers in Sapucaia do Sul and the greater Porto Alegre region (RS, Brazil). Typically mid-career, Portuguese-speaking, searching for a local specialist via Google on mobile. They have a broken piece of equipment or need a scheduled service call, and they want to reach someone trustworthy as fast as possible — usually ending up on WhatsApp within 30 seconds of landing.

## Product Purpose

Convert local search traffic into WhatsApp contacts. The site exists to establish trust quickly (real ratings, real reviews, professional look) and lower the friction to making that first contact. Success = visitor taps the WhatsApp button.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, direct, local. Not flashy tech — workshop credibility. The kind of business where the owner picks up the phone.

## Anti-references

- Generic HVAC SaaS templates (dark blue grid, abstract icons, "AI-powered")
- Overdone glassmorphism or animated hero videos
- Corporate national chains that feel cold and anonymous

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through specificity.** Real names, real ratings, real address. Concrete details outperform abstract claims every time.
2. **Speed above decoration.** Every section should load fast and let the user find the phone or WhatsApp button without hunting.
3. **Local over generic.** Copy and imagery should feel like Sapucaia do Sul, not São Paulo or the US.
4. **Action at every scroll stop.** Each section should have a clear next step — not just information.
5. **Functional states earn their place.** Hover, focus, and error states must work correctly. Half-implemented interaction is worse than none.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Users may be on low-end Android devices on 4G. Touch targets 44px minimum. `prefers-reduced-motion` already handled in code.
